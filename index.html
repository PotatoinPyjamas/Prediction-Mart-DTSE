What is a Prediction Market?

Shares as Probability

Each share is priced between $0 and $1, reflecting the market’s belief in the probability of an event.

Dynamic Odds

Prices shift based on supply/demand. Unlike fixed betting odds, prices evolve with new information.

Settlement

If the event happens (“YES”), shares settle to $1. If not, they settle to $0.

Profit & Loss

If you buy at $0.30 and it settles at $1, your return is 3.33× your stake. If it settles at 0, you lose your stake.
